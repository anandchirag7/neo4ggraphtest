[OVERVIEW]
The LM2576 is a high‑efficiency, wide‑input‑range switching voltage regulator designed for demanding mobile applications. It operates from a supply voltage of 4 V to 24 V and can deliver up to 8 A of load current while maintaining a low quiescent current (< 5 mA). The device is offered in several compact packages: SOT‑223, DFN‑5, SOD‑726 (P), and TSSOP‑16.

[KEY FEATURES]
- **Wide Input Voltage Range:** 4 V – 24 V (±3 V tolerance)  
- **High Output Current Capability:** Up to 8 A continuous load current  
- **Low Quiescent Current:** < 5 mA, suitable for battery‑powered designs  
- **High Efficiency:** Switched‑mode operation with low losses in MOSFETs, magnetic components, and output transistor  
- **Adaptive Load Regulation:** Provides excellent load regulation across the operating range  
- **Compact Footprint:** Available in SOT‑223, DFN‑5, SOD‑726 (P), and TSSOP‑16 packages  
- **Maximum Continuous Input Power:** 15 W (as demonstrated with LTSpice simulation)  

[APPLICATIONS]
- Mobile power supplies
- Battery‑operated equipment
- Portable electronics requiring efficient DC‑DC conversion
- Systems where space and thermal dissipation are critical

[PACKAGE OPTIONS]
- **SOT‑223:** 3‑lead surface‑mount package, commonly used for low‑profile designs.  
- **DFN‑5:** 5‑lead dual‑flat‑no‑lead package, offering a small footprint with thermal pad.  
- **SOD‑726 (P):** 6‑lead small outline diode package, suitable for compact layouts.  
- **TSSOP‑16:** 16‑lead thin‑shrink small‑outline package, providing additional pins for optional functions.
